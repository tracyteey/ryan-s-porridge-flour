Recipes

Ryan's Instant Porridge Recipe

  1. Add 4 tablespoons of flour to a cup or bowl.
  2. Slowly add cold water (less than half a cup) and stir until a smooth paste forms.
  3. Gradually pour in hot water or milk while stirring until desired thickness is reached.
  4. Add your favorite sweetener or flavor.
  5. Your porridge is ready to enjoy!

Ryan's High Fibre Porridge Recipe (2 servings)

  1. Mix 4 tablespoons of porridge flour with 1 cup of cold water to form a paste.
  2. Boil 2 cups of water or milk.
  3. Add the paste while stirring on low heat.
  4. Cook for 7–10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Sweeten to taste and serve hot.
