Ryan's Instant Porridge Recipe

  1. Add 4 tablespoons of flour to a cup or bowl.
  2. Slowly add cold water (less than half a cup) and stir well to form a smooth paste (ensure no lumps remain).
  3. Gradually pour in hot water (or hot milk) while stirring until you reach your desired thickness.
  4. Add your favorite sweetener or flavor.
  5. Your porridge is ready to enjoy!
