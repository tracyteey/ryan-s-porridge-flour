Ryan's Instant Porridge Recipe

  1. Add 4 tablespoons of flour to a cup or bowl.
  2. Slowly add cold water until a smooth paste forms.
  3. Gradually pour in hot water or milk while stirring until desired thickness is reached.
  4. Add your favorite sweetener or flavor.
  5. Your porridge is ready to enjoy!

Ryan's High Fibre Porridge Recipe (2 servings)

  1. Mix 4 tablespoons of porridge flour with 1 cup of cold water to form a paste.
  2. Boil 2 cups of water or milk.
  3. Add the paste while stirring on low heat.
  4. Cook for 7–10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Sweeten to taste and serve hot.

Flavour Variations

Lemorific (Lemon Twist)

  1. Prepare your base porridge recipe.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of lemon zest and 1 tablespoon of fresh lemon juice.
  3. Mix in a drizzle of honey.
  4. Garnish with mint leaves and serve warm.

Coconutrific (Creamy Coconut)

  1. Prepare your base porridge recipe.
  2. Stir in 1/4 cup of coconut milk and 2 tablespoons of shredded coconut.
  3. Add a dash of vanilla extract and a pinch of salt.
  4. Top with toasted coconut flakes.

Banarific (Banana Bliss)

  1. Mash one ripe banana and mix into your prepared porridge.
  2. Add a pinch of cinnamon and a drizzle of maple syrup or honey.
  3. Top with banana slices and chopped walnuts.

Chocorific (Cocoa Delight)

  1. Prepare your base porridge recipe.
  2. Mix in 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder and a handful of chocolate chips.
  3. Add a small pinch of salt.
  4. Stir well and serve with whipped cream if desired.

Nutrific (Nutty Boost)

  1. Prepare your base porridge recipe.
  2. Fold in 2 tablespoons each of chopped nuts and seeds.
  3. Add a handful of dried fruits (raisins or cranberries).
  4. Mix thoroughly and serve.
